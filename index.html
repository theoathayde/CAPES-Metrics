How estratos are assigned

For journals, the estrato shown is the one computed from the Scopus highest-category percentile, following the CAPES Procedimento 2 bands (A1 ≥ 87.5%, A2 ≥ 75%, A3 ≥ 62.5%, A4 ≥ 50%, A5 ≥ 37.5%, A6 ≥ 25%, A7 ≥ 12.5%, A8 < 12.5%). For conferences, the estrato comes from the curated CE-SBC classification (H5-index based, with Top10/Top20 adjustment).
